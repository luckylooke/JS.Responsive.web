Module: detectDeviceOrientation

Detection of device orientation (not orientation of viewport aka width/height ration)
Classes applied to <HTML>:
  • device-orientation-portrait
  • device-orientation-landscape
  • device-orientation-0 - most common state (default for device)
  • device-orientation-90 - device is left side down
  • device-orientation-180 - upside down
  • device-orientation-270 - device is right side down
Events:
  • changedDeviceOrientationAngle
  • changedDeviceOrientation
